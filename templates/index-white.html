CONF 25 - AN INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON

(ICELLL '25)
Literature Review. Research Paper. Research Proposal. Research Page. Research Area.

The conference is organized by the Department of Humanities & Languages, Sona College of Technology in association with the Department of English, Muscat College. This conference provides a global platform for authors to present and share their ideas, research discoveries and insights in the fields of English language, literature and linguistics. The thrust areas include studies on language structure, innovative teaching methods, curriculum development, language acquisition and assessment in the context of teaching English as a second or foreign language. The literature section encompasses the analysis and discussion of various genres, periods and themes in English literature.
